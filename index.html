In 1941, Columbia played its first-ever Homecoming game against Cornell, winning 7-0 at Baker Field.

The Lions have played a Homecoming game every year since (except in 2020, when the season was cancelled due to COVID-19).

Since Al Bagnoli took charge in 2015, Columbia has won 4 and lost 2 Homecoming matches.

Bagnoli brought Columbia Football its biggest Homecoming win ever, with a 38-point win against Penn in 2019.

However, he also brought the program its tied-2nd worst Homecoming loss, with a 35-point loss against Penn in 2015, his first year in charge.

Since the turn of the century, Columbia has only won 5 Homecoming games.

Before Bagnoli won his first Homecoming game in 2016, the last time Columbia won a Homecoming game was in 2000.
